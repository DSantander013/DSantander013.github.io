Inspiration

Why is Champ Bailey a person who inspired me? When I would play football, I played cornerback just like him. I would look up to him because he was one of the greatest Broncos players. I would watch his highlights and study how he would play so I could learn how to become a better cornerback. He was also a great team leader and helped them out so much when they were in a tough spot. Champ Bailey would always try to motivate his team mates when he could, and when they would make little mistakes he would tell them to brush it off.

He was also on the shorter end of cornerbacks in the NFL so I thought if he could make it then so could I. He was so dedicaated and hard working that he came on top. He worked so hard that he even made it into the NFL Hall Of Fame, which is very hard to do. He would show up to play and make big plays for the team and would never give up. He made my day a little brighter when I realized if I worked hard enough I could do it too. He made me really enjoy football a lot more watching him play and wanting to be good. Other people that played with him would say he had a competitive nature aand always wanted to get better. I felt like I was the same as him.
